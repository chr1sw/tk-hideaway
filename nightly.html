Guest House


The amenity-rich Guest House is fully self-contained with all the comforts of home. There are nine fully-furnished rooms that share access to new appliances in a fully equipped kitchen, laundry facilities, and entertainment electronics, including DVD / VCR player connected to Stereo TV. Wireless internet access is available throughout the Guest House. The Talkeetna Hideaway Guesthouse is a perfect spot for hunters, fishermen and other short-term visitors to Talkeetna.

At the completion of Summer 2003, we built the Guest House which is open year round for overnight hostel-style accommodation.
